Marketing purpose

Why this prototype is useful

The prototype makes an abstract idea easier to understand. Instead of only describing surplus-based ownership, the site lets a visitor interact with a simplified model of the planning conversation.

01

It starts with a concrete goal

The visitor chooses something specific: a home, vehicle, truck, workstation, equipment package, or business asset.

02

It reveals the gap

The planner compares the ownership target with the visitor’s direct contribution plan. This makes the planning problem visible.

03

It tells a guided story

The roadmap explains the stages instead of merely displaying dates. This makes the prototype better for marketing and onboarding.

04

It avoids overpromising

The tool does not predict returns, profits, mining output, appreciation, liquidity, or ownership outcomes.

Prototype disclaimer

This Surplus Ownership Planner is a demonstration interface for educational and marketing purposes only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, credit, lending, mining, staking, or accounting advice.

It does not predict or guarantee appreciation, mining output, staking output, liquidity, profitability, returns, financing approval, asset purchase, or ownership outcomes.

All calculations are simplified planning estimates based only on the numbers entered by the visitor. Any real Rousix onboarding, subscription, machine purchase, lease-back discussion, or ownership strategy should be reviewed directly with a qualified Rousix representative and appropriate professional advisors.
