Concept

A clearer way to explain surplus-based planning

This prototype turns an abstract idea into an interactive planning experience. Visitors can see how a goal, contribution base, timeline, and discussion tier fit together.

01

Concrete goal

The user begins with something specific: a home, vehicle, truck, workstation, equipment package, or business asset.

02

Visible gap

The planner shows what the direct contribution path covers and what remains to be discussed.

03

Guided explanation

The roadmap explains each phase in plain language instead of simply showing vague timeline labels.

04

Careful expectations

The demo avoids predictions about returns, liquidity, appreciation, mining output, or ownership outcomes.
