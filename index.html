    Plain-English explanation

    Marketing concept

    How this prototype explains the Rousix idea

    The goal is not to produce a financial prediction. The goal is to make a complex idea feel visual, practical, and easy to discuss.

    01

    Goal identification

    The visitor chooses what they want to own: a home, car, truck, equipment, workstation, or business asset.

    02

    Surplus gap

    The planner shows the difference between the target price and the visitor’s direct contribution plan.

    03

    Roadmap generation

    The app turns the numbers into a simple timeline that can support onboarding and representative-guided planning.

    04

    Clear expectations

    The interface avoids return promises and keeps the demo framed as an educational planning tool.

    Prototype disclaimer

    This Surplus Ownership Planner is a demonstration interface for educational and marketing purposes only. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, credit, lending, mining, staking, or accounting advice. It does not predict appreciation, mining output, staking output, liquidity, profitability, returns, or ownership outcomes.

    All calculations are simplified planning estimates based only on the numbers entered by the visitor. Any real Rousix onboarding, subscription, machine purchase, lease-back discussion, or ownership strategy should be reviewed directly with a qualified Rousix representative and appropriate professional advisors.
